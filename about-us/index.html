It all started so simply.

FIRST WE LIKED TOFU.

BUT THEN WE WANTED SOMETHING MORE.

01

Something new, something ours, something organic. Something full of flavour and full of colour. So we started peeling, chopping, mixing and grinding. We tried everything to get all the sharpness out of ginger and all the heat of our chillies. Imagine to cook a huge pot of garlic. That smell. And then sketching, drawing, colouring, printing and glueing our first packagings. First three, then five and finally the eight colours. It took us a good year, but here they are. And naturally, some more are on the way. Can’t wait for the purple one to see the light of day!

02

WE WOULD LIKE YOU TO BE CREATIVE.

We want you to be brave and bold. Have fun! Impress with simplicity or with a five-course dinner.

Take them all apart. Squash, grind, carve. Add smoke and fire and colour. Add heat. But never forget about freshness. Can be just a quick fix on the go, something to fuel your workout or even a breakfast after a wild night. So many possibilities, buy don’t worry, we’ll help you do it. If we are able to bring just a bit of extra colour in your kitchens, we’ll be more than happy.
03
